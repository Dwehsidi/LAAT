About LAAT

Our Story & Purpose

The Liberian Association of Austin Texas (LAAT) was established to serve as the primary civic organization for Liberians and friends of Liberia in Central Texas. From our earliest days, LAAT has been a home away from home — a place where culture is kept alive, voices are heard, and families are supported.

We believe that a strong community is built on unity, transparency, and shared purpose. Through advocacy, outreach, cultural programs, and welfare services, LAAT continues to build a foundation for generations to come.

Investing in the next generation of Liberian-Americans through scholarships, mentorship, and leadership development.

Support Services

Community Welfare & Support

LAAT stands with members in times of need. Our welfare programs provide a safety net for Liberian families in Austin.

🆘

Emergency Assistance

Immediate financial and logistical support for members facing unexpected hardship or crisis situations.

🕊️

Bereavement Support

Compassionate support for families during loss, including coordination of community condolences and memorial services.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Assistance

Programs to support new arrivals, single-parent families, and members navigating major life transitions.

📋

Immigration Resources

Referrals to trusted immigration attorneys and legal aid organizations serving the Liberian community.

🧠

Counseling Referrals

Connecting members with culturally informed mental health and counseling professionals in Austin.

🏥

Health & Wellness

Health fairs, screenings, and partnerships with local clinics to support member wellbeing.
